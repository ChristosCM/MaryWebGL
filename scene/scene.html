Controls

W/S:

Camera: Move Forward and Back

A/D:

Camera: Move Left and Right (Looks at the object)

Q/E:

Camera: Look Pivot Left and Pivot Right

T:

Stop tracking the object

Mouse:

Click and move to rotate the object

L:

Toggle lights on and off

Arrow Keys:

Move the purple kite

Space:

Stop the bird

Note:

The trees seem like the go back and forth but that is an optical illusion. Look at them from the top to see them rotate normally
